Rules for Visitors

Digital Museum, Assam Legislative Assembly

Visitor Guidelines
  1. All visitors will be provided entry from Gate No.14, Assam Legislative Assembly.
  2. All visitors must report at Gate No.14 at least 15 minutes before the allotted time slot.
  3. All visitors must carry a valid identification card as mentioned in the registration form and produce the same on demand to the security personnel.
  4. Following IDs are acceptable: Passport, Voter ID Card, Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Driving License, Government Employee ID Card or any ID issued by Government.
  5. All visitors and members must pass through a security check.
  6. All visitors will be frisked before entering the premises.
  7. Any kind of firearm, ammunition, inflammable material, or sharp object is strictly prohibited.
  8. Smoking, consumption or carrying tobacco, chewing gum, etc. is strictly prohibited.
  9. Water or any liquid items are not allowed inside the Digital Museum.
  10. All visitors must adhere to the instructions given by Assam Legislative officials.
  11. Camera/Mobiles may be used only at designated places with permission from officials.
  12. The guided tour will be suspended one week prior to commencement of the Assam Legislative Assembly session and remain suspended for three days after the session.
  13. The amount once paid will not be refunded.

• Please follow all rules for smooth entry

• Management reserves the right to deny entry
